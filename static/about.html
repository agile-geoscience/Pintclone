About locati.fi

What? This web app tries to find the locations of things you 'pin' from the Internet.

Who?Made by Anneya Golob, Diego Castaneda, and Matt Hall at the Unearthed Hackathon in Toronto, 3-5 March 2017.

How?You probably need the Chrome extension.

Why?We all need a bit more geospatial in our lives.
